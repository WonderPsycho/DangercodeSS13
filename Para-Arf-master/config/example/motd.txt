<h1>Welcome to ARFS Dallus!</h1>
<p>A.R.F.S. Dallus is a furry, roleplaying SS13 server with medium-high roleplaying standards. We focus on having a large race variety with the creative choice to make your original characters. We have a friendly community and staff who would love to help you find your place here!
<br /><br>

<i>This server is running <a href="http://nanotrasen.se/">Paradise's</a> modification of the <a href="http://baystation12.net/">Baystation 12</a> and <a href="http://ss13.eu/">/tg/station13</a> SS13 code </i>
